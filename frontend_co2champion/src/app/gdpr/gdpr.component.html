Privacy Policy (GDPR)

General Information

Protecting your personal data is a priority for us at CO2Champion. This policy explains how we handle your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data We Collect

We only collect the data you voluntarily provide to us. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Your email address
  • Your company information
  • Reports or other inputs you actively submit

How We Use Your Data

The data you provide is only used to:

  • Display information relevant to your use of the CO2Champion website
  • Provide you with reports, rankings, or other requested functionality

We do not use your data for any purpose beyond those directly related to our website's functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties unless explicitly required by law.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Restriction: Request a restriction of processing
  • Objection: Object to data processing
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another provider
